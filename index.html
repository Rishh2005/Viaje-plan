Insights for Paris

Must-visit attractions:

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Arc de Triomphe

Local customs:

Greet people with "Bonjour" (hello) and "Merci" (thank you). It's polite to learn a few basic French phrases.

Transportation options:

Paris has an extensive public transportation system including the Metro, RER trains, and buses. Consider purchasing a Navigo pass for unlimited travel.

Dining recommendations:

  • Le Chateaubriand - Modern French cuisine
  • L'Ami Louis - Classic French bistro
  • Clown Bar - Innovative small plates
  • Le Baratin - Rustic French cooking
